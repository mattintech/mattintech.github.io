Size Tips

Retail Kiosks

Most retail kiosks use 1080x1920 (portrait) or 1920x1080 (landscape). High-end displays may need 4K resolution.

Android Phones

Modern Android phones typically use 1080x2400 or higher. Flagship devices like Galaxy S24 Ultra use 1440x3088.

Android Tablets

Tablet resolutions vary widely. Common sizes: 1600x2560 for 10-11" tablets, 1848x2960 for larger displays.

Samsung Rugged Devices

XCover7 Pro uses 1080x2408. Tab Active5 is 1920x1200 (8"), Tab Active5 Pro is 2560x1600 (10.1"). Great for industrial/field use.

Digital Signage

For professional signage, use 3840x2160 (4K) to ensure crisp text and images on large displays.

Pro Tip

Always design at the target resolution. Avoid upscaling as it causes blurriness. PNG for graphics with transparency, JPG for photos.
